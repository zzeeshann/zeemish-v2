Your body does thousands of things every day without asking you. Breathing, digesting, balancing, repairing. Most of it works. Some of it doesn't work as well as it could. This course is about the gap between what your body does automatically and what happens when you pay attention on purpose.

No wellness jargon. No miracle fixes. Just what the research says, explained plainly, with practice where it helps.
